Male Infertility Management

Book Appointment

Specialized diagnosis and treatment for male fertility issues. We understand that infertility affects both partners and provide comprehensive, confidential care for men's reproductive health.

Our Approach:

  • Semen analysis and sperm function tests
  • Hormone testing and assessment
  • Genetic screening and counseling
  • Medical and surgical treatment options
  • Lifestyle and nutritional guidance
  • Sperm retrieval procedures (TESA/PESA)

"Male factor infertility accounts for about 40% of fertility challenges. Our specialized team provides comprehensive evaluation and treatment in a supportive, confidential environment."
